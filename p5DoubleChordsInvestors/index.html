Top 100 Investors and Companies

Explore the relationships between the top investors and the top companies through their investments/funding, with this interactive visualization.

  • A list of clickable companies, with a visual representation of the amount they received.
  • A list of clickable investors, with a visual representation of the amount they funded.
  • Hovering a company highlights its investors, hovering an investor highlights its companies.
  • Clicking on a company shows a list with all of their investments and corresponding amounts.
  • Clicking on an investor shows a list with all of their investments and corresponding amounts.

Data Source:

Investments data collected from Crunchbase for 2007 to 2016.
